## Welcome

**Clariti** is an AI-powered specification tool for structural engineers and architects working with construction products. Clariti is quickly adding products to the platform, to find what is availble please visit [clariti.site](http://clariti.site) 

### Key Benefits

- **Standards-compliant calculations** — Full calculation verification with transparent methodology
- **Intelligent optimization** — AI acts as an expert guide
- **Real-time 3D visualization** — See your connections in context before specifying
- **Instant documentation** — Generate professional specification reports automatically

## Standards Coverage

Clariti currently supports design verification to:

- **EN 1992-4:2018** — Design of fastenings for use in concrete
